## What Is OneDrive for Mac

on your computer and keeps everything inside it synchronized between your Mac, your other computers and
OneDrive.com. Whenever you add, change or delete a file in one location, every other location is updated
to match, so the version on your desk and the version in the cloud are always the same.

Rather than bolting a web page onto the desktop, <a href="#">cloud storage mac</a> integrates directly
with the Finder. Your OneDrive files appear like any other folder on the Mac, which means the tools and
habits you already use — dragging, copying, organizing into folders — apply to your cloud storage too.
For anyone who works across a laptop, a desktop and the web, this Finder-native approach turns OneDrive
into a natural extension of the Mac rather than a separate app you have to remember to open.
